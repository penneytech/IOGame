Challenge cards

Try one of these the next time you sit down to design a character. They get easier or harder depending on the deck. Mix and match!

Two powers only

Build a character with exactly two powers. They must feel different — e.g. one offensive, one defensive.

Color array

Use a JS array of colors and pick one with Math.random(). Same character, fresh paint each round.

Power factory

Write a function that builds a power from arguments (e.g. makeBolt(damage, cd)). Use it 2+ times.

Loop of three

Use a for loop to generate three related powers (different keys, increasing damage and cooldown).

Support build

Make a character whose main job is helping teammates (heals, regen area, slow on enemies, weak attacks).

Under 90 points

Total cost must be ≤ 90 in the balance report. Same fun, more headroom.

Named weakness

Pick one weakness on purpose (low HP, long cooldowns, no mobility). Write it in your character's comment header.

Fairer cooldown

Take an existing build, find the strongest power, and double its cooldownMs. Re-test against a friend.

Tradeoff swap

Trade 20 points from one stat to another (e.g. drop HP, raise damage). Did the character feel better or worse?

If/else build

In buildCharacter(), use an if to swap between an "aggressive" and "defensive" version of your character.

Combo finisher

Design a 2-power combo: one power sets up (slow / stun / knockback), the other finishes the kill.

Playtest journal

After 3 rounds, answer in a comment: What worked? What was unfair? What will you change?
